Moderation Guidelines

Content is considered inappropriate if it contains:

  • Explicit sexual content or pornography
  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Hate speech or discriminatory language
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Personal identifying information without consent

Content violates copyright if it:

  • Contains copyrighted material without proper attribution
  • Reproduces entire works without permission
  • Circumvents digital rights management
  • Distributes pirated software, media, or content

Content is considered harmful if it:

  • Promotes dangerous activities that could lead to physical harm
  • Contains instructions for illegal activities
  • Advocates for self-harm or suicide
  • Spreads malware or phishing content
  • Promotes dubious health advice that could endanger users
